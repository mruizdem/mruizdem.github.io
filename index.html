A Little About Me

I'm thrilled to be on this coding journey, and I can't wait to dive into the world of development. It all started with a curiosity that turned into a passion, and now, I'm excited to see where it takes me. When I'm not coding, you'll often find me behind a drum kit, learning and losing myself in the music. I'm always on the lookout for new bands and I share my home with two adorable sugar gliders, and have love for any animals. Whether it's my sugar gliders, dogs, cats, or any furry friend, I'm a true animal lover at heart.

And speaking of passions, let's talk about coffee! I'm a bona fide coffee nerd. You can quiz me about coffee beans, brewing methods, or even ask me about my favorite brew. I take my coffee seriously, and can even tell you the region of a coffee just by sipping it. Don't hesitate to ask me for coffee recommendations — I've got plenty! In my downtime, I also play plenty of video games. I'm always on the lookout for new games to play and enjoy immersing myself in different gaming worlds. If you have any game recommendations, feel free to share them with me — I'm all ears.

Thanks for stopping by, and I'm looking forward to connecting with you, whether it's about coding, music, animals, coffee, or gaming!
